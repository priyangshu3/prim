Our Story

Welcome to G&G
We are passionate about serving authentic Indian cuisine made with fresh, high-quality ingredients. Our story began with a love of Indian food and a desire to share it with others.We have also spent years perfecting our recipes, ensuring that every dish is flavorful and authentic.

Our menu features a wide variety of dishes, from classic favorites like chicken tikka masala and butter chicken to more regional specialties like biryani and vindaloo. We also offer a variety of vegetarian and vegan options. We believe that everyone should feel safe and welcome in our restaurant.

Meet Our Chef

The chef is the heart of the kitchen, responsible for creating the dishes that make the restaurant special. A good chef must have a deep understanding of ingredients, flavors, and cooking techniques. They must also be able to work under pressure, manage a team, and create a positive dining experience for guests.

The chef's role is not just to cook food. They must also be able to develop new recipes, create menus, and manage the kitchen budget. They must also be able to work with other members of the restaurant team, such as the front-of-house staff, to ensure that the dining experience is seamless.
